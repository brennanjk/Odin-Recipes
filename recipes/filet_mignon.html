Bacon-Wrapped Filet Mignon

Seared and oven cooked bacon-wrapped filet mignon recipe

Mouth watering filet mignon recipe that's quick and easy to make. We'll be searing the filet with an ovenproof cast iron skillet, then finishing the filet in the oven.

Ingredients:

  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 beef filet steak, 8 to 10 ounces, 2 inches thick
  • 1 piece thin bacon
  • 1 tablespoon butter, more if needed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
